GMTK Game Jam 2025

Theme: Loop

This game was made in 4 days for the GMTK game jam with help from my sister. Special thanks to the Elizabeth Warren Caricature by Veg3D! Here is our ranking out of 9416 other submissions:

  • Creativity #923 4.0336
  • Enjoyment #1082 3.679
  • Audio #2038 3.214
  • Narrative #2478 2.679
  • Artwork #3519 3.143

Play this game if you ever wished you were belly dancing as Elizabeth Warren! Move your hips in various loop patterns to earn coins or just dance for free in creative mode.

If we had more time we would add different loop types (like an infinity symbol, square, etc), different body parts to control, different characters and environments to choose from, more songs, and maybe throw in some unexpected challenges.
